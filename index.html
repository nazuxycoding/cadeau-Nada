A Poem for My Strong Sister

You stand so tall, a fortress in the gale,
A silent strength that never seems to fail.
You hold the world upon your shoulders wide,
With all the pressure hidden deep inside.

But Nada, listen, you don't have to fight alone,
You don't need a heart carved out of stone.
I love your strength, it’s true, I always will,
But I’m here for the moments when the world is still.

Show me the tears, the cracks, the weary sigh,
You can be vulnerable beneath this sky.
For in your weakness, I find my place to be,
The shield for you, as you have been for me.
