A Poem for My Dear Sister

Through my eyes, every detail is a grace,
The way your curly hair frames your face.
That charming smile that lights up the room,
Like a flower in its most beautiful bloom.

I see the depth in your piercing eyes,
And how you carry yourself, so brave and wise.
Your warm embrace is my safest place,
A gift of love that time can't erase.

You stand so strong, a fortress in the gale,
But even the strongest can feel a bit frail.
Don't hide the tears or the weary sigh,
You can be vulnerable beneath this sky.

For I am here to hold you, to listen, to stay,
To be your shield at the end of the day.
